VhoTel Facilities

Sport Center


To help maintain your exercise regime even when away from home, VhoTel boasts a well-equipped fitness centre with a wide range of up-to-date equipment and is overseen by certified instructors.

Swimming Pool


Relax and rejuvenate at VhoTel heated swimming pool at 5th floor, perfect for a refreshing dip with stunning views. Our heated kids pool is safe and fun for little ones, while our Jacuzzi provides the ultimate relaxation.

Sauna


Unwind at our fully equipped sauna and steam room for the ultimate bliss. They cater to both male and female guests. Take some time out to enjoy this tranquil space, where you can relax at your leisure and feel rested and renewed.

Billiard


Relax and challenge yourself to a game of billiards while waiting for your family to explore the facilities at VhoTel.

Restaurant and Bar


Situated overlooking the surrounding verdant valley, the new concept is designed to enhance your dining experience, be it breakfast, lunch, afternoon tea, or whilst sharing a romantic dinner and the delightful views.
